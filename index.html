SOME OF WORCKS

MOONFLOWER $ NIGHT SKETCHES

A delicate moonflower in full bloom, its petals unfolding gracefully under the night sky. The soft shading and fine pen strokes capture its ethereal beauty.

BLOOMTIME $ TULIP SKETCHES

Drawings that capture the delicacy of tulips in their fullest expression. Each piece depicts a tulip in full bloom, with petals unfolding gently under soft light.

ROSEBLOOM $ ROYAL SKETCHES

Features detailed drawings of roses, showcasing their regal elegance. With delicate lines and rich shading.

FLORALART $ HIBISCUS SKETCH

Presents the striking form of hibiscus flowers in black and white.

ROSEBUNCH $ VIVID BLOSSOMS

The sketch highlights the intricate layers and dramatic presence of each petal.

PINK PETALS ✿ BLOOMING ELEGANCE

A delicate bouquet of flowers painted in soft pink watercolors. Gentle strokes bring the petals to life with an ethereal elegance, while light and shadow add depth and texture. The artwork captures nature’s grace in a harmonious and expressive composition.

INKBLOOM ✦ SHADOWED PETALS

A graceful arrangement of flowers in gentle pink hues, brought to life with delicate watercolor strokes. The interplay of light and shadow adds dimension, crafting a serene and captivating composition that reflects the soft, timeless beauty of nature.

CHARCOAL BLOSSOM ✦ SOFT WHISPERS

Softly sketched flowers in charcoal, where deep shadows and gentle highlights shape a quiet, dreamlike atmosphere, evoking nature’s delicate grace with timeless charm.
